Application Procedure

Obtain an application form from the Registrar’s office or from the liaison offices. Fill the form correctly and attach copies of all your education certificates and documents along with the application fee as stipulated in the application form. Return these documents promptly to the Registrar’s Office at least 3 weeks before the beginning of the new academic year. If your application is accepted, you will receive a notification-of-acceptance letter from the Registrar. You may come to the university only if you have already received the acceptance letter.
